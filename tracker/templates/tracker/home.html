Frequently Asked Questions

Is it normal to feel cramps?

Yes, cramps are common and happen due to your body releasing chemicals that help shed the uterine lining.

How long will my period last?

Periods typically last 3-7 days, but each person is different. Track it over time to understand your cycle.

What should I do if my period is late?

Periods can fluctuate, especially if you're young or stressed. If you're worried, consult a healthcare provider.
